# LearnTok

*Scroll to Learn. One Sip at a Time.*

## Problem

Technical learning is broken for busy people. Microsoft Learn content is comprehensive but designed for long, focused reading sessions. In reality, engineers learn in fragments: five minutes between meetings, a commute, a lunch break. There is no experience that meets learners where their time actually is.

## Solution

LearnTok is an AI powered, scroll based learning experience that transforms any Microsoft Learn topic into a personalized feed of sip sized learning cards. Each sip is a 30 to 60 second read. The feed adapts in real time: topics you engage with expand into deeper content, topics you skip fade away. You're always in control, and you never have to commit more than a minute at a time.

## How It Works

1. **Start with a spark.** Enter a topic ("Azure Functions") or paste a Microsoft Learn URL.
2. **AI breaks it down.** Azure OpenAI analyzes the subject, maps out a learning path, and generates a stream of sip sized cards.
3. **Scroll and react.** Swipe through sips like a feed. Heart what interests you and the AI queues deeper content on that thread. Skip what doesn't grab you.
4. **Resume anytime.** Your progress is saved. Come back tomorrow and pick up exactly where you left off, or rewind to revisit earlier concepts.

## What Makes It Different

**Adaptive, not static.** The feed reshapes itself based on your reactions. This is not a fixed course or a chatbot. It is a living curriculum that evolves with your curiosity.

**Designed for real schedules.** No hour long commitments. Every sip stands on its own.

**Grounded in real content.** Powered by Azure AI Search over Microsoft Learn, so the knowledge is authoritative, not hallucinated.

## Target Users

Engineers, PMs, and technical learners inside Microsoft (and eventually external) who want to stay current on Azure services, frameworks, and best practices without carving out dedicated study time.

## Tech Stack

Azure OpenAI · Azure AI Search · Cosmos DB · Next.js · Express · TypeScript

## Elevator Pitch

Learning is fragmented now. Your time comes in small windows between meetings, on the bus, waiting in line. LearnTok turns those spare minutes into your smartest minutes. Enter any topic or Microsoft Learn link and get a personalized, infinite scroll feed of AI generated learning sips that adapt to your curiosity in real time. It is TikTok for technical learning: effortless to start, impossible to forget where you left off.
